alt attribute

The alt attribute is required. A web page will not validate correctly without it. (not enforced)

<img src="NO_IMAGE.jpg" alt="Chloe and cooper cuddling (alt attribute)" width=400px height=300px>

width and height, or style?

<img src="chloe_and_cooper.jpg" alt="Chloe and cooper cuddling" width=400px height=300px>
<img src="chloe_and_cooper.jpg" alt="Chloe and cooper cuddling" style="width:400px;height:300px;">
